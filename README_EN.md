# Routiny
Small Routines, Big Changes 🌱

## About
Routiny is a habit tracking app that's easy to start.
It's okay to not be perfect. We'll go at your pace, one step at a time.

## Key Features
- ✅ **Easy Habit Setup** - Customize your habits with emojis and colors
- 🌱 **Grow Plants** - Complete routines and watch your plant grow
- 📱 **Home Screen Widget** - Check off habits without opening the app
- 🌙 **Rest for Today** - Take a day off when you need it
- ⏸️ **Pause** - Take a longer break when life gets busy
- 📅 **Calendar View** - See your habit records at a glance
- 📊 **Statistics** - Track how consistent you've been
- 🔔 **Gentle Reminders** - Friendly notifications to keep you on track
- 🏅 **Collection** - Collect completed plants in your garden
- 🎯 **First 7 Days Mission** - Special missions and rewards for new users
